# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in the OIDC SSO Toolkit!

## Read-Only Repository

This repository is a **read-only mirror** of an internal development repository. We do not accept pull requests or direct contributions to this repository.

### Why Read-Only?

This toolkit is maintained internally by Candescent and synchronized to this public repository. All development, testing, and releases are managed through our internal processes.

### How to Provide Feedback

If you have:
- **Bug reports** - Please open an Issue describing the problem
- **Feature requests** - Please open an Issue describing the use case
- **Questions** - Please open a Discussion or contact your Candescent representative

### For Partners and Financial Institutions

If you are a Candescent partner or FI integrating with our OIDC SSO:
- Contact your Candescent Project Manager for support
- Refer to our [Documentation Site](https://docs.candescent.com) for documentation
- Follow the [Step-by-Step Integration Instructions](https://docs.candescent.com/guides/OIDC%20Integration/overview/#step-by-step-integration-process)
